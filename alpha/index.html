IDM241 Alpha

Microinteraction: Ikea 'Add to Bag' Button - Ikea Website

Triggers

  • Hover on/off shopping bag icon
  • Click on shopping bag icon

Rules

Hover over shopping bag icon

  • Cursor turns to pointer
  • Button will turn a darker shade of blue
  • Name of the product will become underlined

Click on shopping bag icon

  • Button turns an even darker shade of blue while mouse click is down
  • Button returns to previous shade of blue once mouse click is released
  • Shopping bag icon is replaced by a circle that bounces while item is being added to bag
  • Circle is then replaced by a check mark that slides up
  • Shopping bag icon in the top right of page increases its number by 1
  • Tab pops up on the top right that says product was added, along with a "Show" and close button
  • After a 1.5s, shopping bag icon reappears
