Triggers

  • Hover on/off circular blue shopping bag icon

Rules

Hover over shopping bag icon

  • Cursor turns to pointer
  • Button expands slightly and turns a darker shade of blue
  • Name of the product will become underlined immediately
  • Gray tooltip that says "Add to Bag" pops out and expands from above the button

Hover off shopping bag icon

  • Pointer turns back to cursor
  • Button shrinks to original size and turns back to original, lighter blue color
  • Underline under the name of product disappears immediately
  • Gray tooltip that says "Add to Bag" shrinks back above the button
